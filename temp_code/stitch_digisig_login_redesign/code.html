The Digisig Project

Digisig is an experimental resource designed to assist in the discovery and study of historical seals. It brings together data from a wide variety of sources, including museum catalogues, archival finding aids, and archaeological reports.

Our mission is to provide a unified platform for sigillography research, enabling scholars to analyze the complex relationships between seal matrices, impressions, and the individuals or institutions they represented.

By digitizing and linking these rich datasets, we preserve a critical part of our cultural heritage for future generations of historians and researchers.
