The Digisig Project

Seals are an important part of cultural heritage, but they can be difficult for researchers to discover and access using the existing reference works, catalogues and finding aids. Digisig helps researchers discover what seals survive, determine where they are located, and find out who has recorded and described them.

By digitizing and linking these rich datasets, we preserve a critical part of our cultural heritage for future generations of historians and researchers.
